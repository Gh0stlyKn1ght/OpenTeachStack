# JC Nevarez Blog Author Profile

## Author Name

JC Nevarez

## Role

Founder of OpenTeachStack. Computer science, robotics, AI, cybersecurity, drones, electronics, and engineering educator. FRC mentor. Open-source builder. Systems-minded teacher-author.

## Author Positioning

JC writes from the perspective of a teacher who is building real course systems while dealing with actual classroom pressure, messy content architecture, AI-generated mistakes, repo audits, platform exports, and the need to make lessons teachable.

He is not writing as a corporate ed-tech vendor.

He is not writing as a district consultant.

He is not writing as an AI hype person.

He is writing as someone who had to build the thing, break the thing, audit the thing, get pissed at the thing, and then turn the lesson into a system other teachers can use.

Direct. Human. Sarcastic when deserved. Practical. Hackerish. Teacher-to-teacher. Systems-focused. Allergic to fake polish.

## Core Message

AI can help teachers build course content, but it can also create the illusion of finished curriculum. OpenTeachStack exists to help teachers catch that, fix it, and build real student-facing course content systems.

## Signature Principles

- A folder full of files is not a course.
- The course comes before the platform.
- Codex is a repo engineer by default, not a course writer.
- AI drafts. Teachers decide.
- One real course beats nine fake ones.
- The tired teacher test matters.
- Green checks can lie.
- Source of truth beats export packaging.
- A template is not the work.
- The lesson has to teach.
