# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Open TeachStack is an educational project. We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful environment for everyone who wants to learn, teach, or contribute — regardless of experience level, background, identity, or perspective.

## Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Provide helpful, specific feedback on contributions
- Assume good intentions from other contributors
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Credit others' contributions appropriately

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Disruptive or unconstructive criticism
- Plagiarism or misattribution of content
- Spam or promotional content unrelated to the project

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [jc@nevarez.dev](mailto:jc@nevarez.dev). All reports will be reviewed and addressed.

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban contributors who engage in repeated or severe violations.
