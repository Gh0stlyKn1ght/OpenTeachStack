JC Nevarez is a computer science, robotics, AI, and engineering educator building OpenTeachStack from real classroom pressure.

His work focuses on helping teachers turn messy curriculum expectations, AI drafts, source materials, assignments, rubrics, and platform constraints into student-facing course content systems that are teachable, verifiable, safe, and reusable.

JC writes the OpenTeachStack blog as a build journal: direct, practical, systems-minded, occasionally sarcastic when the system deserves it, and allergic to fake completeness.
