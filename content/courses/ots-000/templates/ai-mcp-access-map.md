## Risk Label

Choose one:

- Low: explanation or drafting only.
- Medium: suggestions inside a file.
- High: repo edits, commands, or external connections.
- Not approved: unknown access or private data risk.

## Review Rule

```text
I will not accept AI work until I have checked:
- scope
- changed files
- diff
- verification step
- privacy boundary
- student-facing impact
```
