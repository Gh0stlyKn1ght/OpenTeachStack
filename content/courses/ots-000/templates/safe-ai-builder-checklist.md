## Stop Conditions

Stop if:

- unrelated files change
- private data appears
- command effect is unclear
- source of truth is uncertain
- generated content replaces authored work
- verification fails
- published route behaves differently than expected

## Final Decision

Choose one:

- Safe to continue.
- Needs revision before continuing.
- Stop and ask for review.
- Do not use this AI workflow.
