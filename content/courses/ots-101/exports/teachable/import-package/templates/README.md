# OTS-101 templates

Artifact templates and reusable course documents.

This directory belongs to `ots-101` and should travel with the course during future migration or export work.

OTS-101 is the active foundations rebuild. Templates may reference local tool, access, source, platform, safety, or boundary notes when they help a reviewer understand the packet, but the templates should stay focused on the Mini Course Content Packet and its review evidence.
