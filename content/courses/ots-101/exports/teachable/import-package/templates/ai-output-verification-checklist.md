# AI Output Verification Checklist

## Purpose

Use this before students see AI-assisted content.

## When to use it

Use it after AI drafts or revises any student-facing lesson, assignment, rubric, quiz, example, or explanation.

## Quality check

- Every risky claim has a source or a teacher verification note.
- Anything uncertain is marked revise or reject.
- Anything that cannot be checked yet is marked blocked.
- The teacher makes the final decision.

## Safety note

AI output is draft material. Do not publish unverified safety, health, legal, policy, or identity-related claims.
