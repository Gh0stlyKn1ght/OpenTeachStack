## Why This Matters

Why this section matters: reliable teaching starts with a system that is clear, inspectable, and reusable after the lesson is over.
## Core Idea

The core idea is to make teaching artifacts visible, reusable, and aligned to student-facing evidence.

## Common Mistake

A common mistake is treating the visible artifact or activity as proof that the teaching system is ready. The reviewer should look for the classroom purpose, artifact evidence, privacy or safety boundaries, and a clear next revision trigger.
