# Build Artifact: Course Content Safety and Access Checklist

In this lesson, you will build a checklist for reviewing course content before students see it.

This checklist should be short enough to use and specific enough to catch real problems.

## What the checklist covers

Your checklist should include:

- student privacy
- safe links and tools
- sensitive prompts
- readable structure
- accessibility supports
- source quality
- copyright or reuse concerns
- teacher verification notes


This connects the checklist to a real piece of content.

## Step 2: Add safety checks

```text
Safety:
[ ] No private student data is visible.
[ ] Examples are fictionalized or safe to share.
[ ] Links open and match the intended destination.
[ ] External tools are appropriate for students and school context.
[ ] The task does not require unnecessary personal disclosure.
[ ] Any safety boundary students need is stated clearly.
```

## Step 3: Add accessibility checks

```text
Accessibility:
[ ] The page uses headings to organize sections.
[ ] Lists are formatted as real bullets or numbered steps.
[ ] Directions are written in student-facing language.
[ ] Materials, links, files, and submission steps are easy to find.
[ ] Images, videos, charts, or diagrams have explanation or alternative access when needed.
[ ] Students know what to do if they get stuck.
```

## Step 4: Add source quality checks

```text
Source quality:
[ ] Important facts are checked against trusted sources.
[ ] AI summaries match the original source meaning.
[ ] Citations or links actually support the claims.
[ ] Sources are appropriate for the grade, topic, and task.
[ ] Sources are accessible to students without unnecessary barriers.
```

## Step 5: Add copyright and reuse checks

```text
Copyright and reuse:
[ ] Materials are teacher-created, school-approved, openly licensed, public domain, linked, excerpted appropriately, or otherwise appropriate for the context.
[ ] Images, media, and excerpts include attribution or source notes when needed.
[ ] Paid, protected, or restricted materials are not reposted carelessly.
[ ] AI was not asked to recreate protected work as a substitute for permission or licensing.
```

## Step 6: Add a decision

```text
Review decision:
[ ] Ready after teacher edits
[ ] Revise and review again
[ ] Do not publish yet
```
