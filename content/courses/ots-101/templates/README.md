# OTS-101 templates

Artifact templates and reusable course documents.

This directory belongs to `ots-101` and should travel with the course during future migration or export work.

OTS-101 follows OTS-000. Templates may reference OTS-000 context when it clarifies tools, access, source, platform, safety, or local boundaries, but the templates should stay focused on the Mini Course Content Packet and its review evidence.
