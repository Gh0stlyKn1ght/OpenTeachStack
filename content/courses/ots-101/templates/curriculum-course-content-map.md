# Curriculum vs Course Content Map

## Purpose

Use this to prevent AI from mixing learning-plan decisions with student-facing materials.

## When to use it

Use it before drafting a lesson, assignment, module page, or LMS unit.

Use it before building export packages for Teachable, Canvas, Google Classroom, or a course website.

## Quality check

- The curriculum decision is observable.
- The student-facing content is something students actually use.
- Teacher-only material is not mixed into the student view.
- Platform or export rows point back to source course content.
- OTS-000 context clarifies tools, access, source, platform, or safety without replacing the curriculum decision.

## Safety note

When safety routines are involved, verify the sequence against local policy and approved equipment guidance before students use it.
