# Revision Log

## Purpose

Use this to track what changed after AI drafting, teacher review, peer review, or classroom use.

## When to use it

Use it whenever course content changes after a draft has been shared or reviewed.

## Blank fields

| Date | Item revised | Reason | Evidence | Next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  |  |  |  |  |

## Completed fictional example

| Date | Item revised | Reason | Evidence | Next check |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2026-06-26 | Startup checklist wording | Students confused "enable" and "power on" | Exit-ticket comments | Review after next practice |

## Quality check

- The reason names a real classroom signal.
- The evidence is inspectable.
- The next check is dated or tied to a teaching moment.

## Safety note

Do not include student names, grades, or private behavior notes in public revision logs.
