# Student-Facing Lesson Page Template

## Purpose

Use this to draft a lesson page students can use without a second private explanation from the teacher.

## When to use it

Use it when publishing a lesson to an LMS, course website, handout, or course hub.

## Completed fictional example

- Title: Safe Robot Startup and Shutdown
- Learning target: I can follow the startup and shutdown checklist before using the robot.
- Why this matters: Robots can move unexpectedly when setup steps are skipped.
- What you need: Class checklist, powered-off practice robot, pencil.
- Steps: Read the checklist, label the scenario, practice the routine with the teacher watching.
- Worked example: In the sample scenario, the student checks the enable switch before touching controls.
- Quick check: Which step prevents accidental motor movement?
- Submit: One labeled scenario and one sentence explaining the safest decision.
- What to do if stuck: Ask your team lead to point to the next checklist step.

## Quality check

- Students can identify the first action.
- The example matches the task.
- The submission is specific.
- The page does not expose teacher-only notes.

## Safety note

Use only approved safety steps and age-appropriate language. Do not include real student names or private incident details.
