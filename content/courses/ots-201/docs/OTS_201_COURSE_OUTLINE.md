## Course Thesis

Google Workspace becomes more useful when teachers treat Drive, Docs, Sheets, Slides, Forms, and Calendar as one operating system instead of a pile of separate tools.

OTS-201 stays non-automation-first. It prepares teachers to organize files, templates, trackers, routines, and maintenance habits before moving into Apps Script or public course publishing.

## Final Artifact

Workspace command center

The final artifact should include:

- Drive architecture map
- reusable Docs or Slides delivery template
- curriculum tracker sheet
- Forms and Calendar operations workflow

## Release Boundaries

OTS-201 should not require Apps Script, GitHub, domains, or public publishing. Those belong in later pathway courses.

Keep the course focused on teacher-owned organization, recoverability, accessibility, and handoff clarity.
