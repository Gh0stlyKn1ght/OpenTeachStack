# Email Safety and Rate Limits

## Why This Matters

Automation is ready when a teacher can point to the decision, the classroom use, the evidence, and the next review step.

In **Forms, Email, and Reminders**, the work sits inside teacher automation with Google Apps Script and Workspace files. The risk is automating a process before the trigger, input, output, owner, permission boundary, and failure path are clear. This lesson narrows that risk into one visible update to **Reminder workflow**.

## Core Idea

Treat this as a boundary lesson. Name what should be shared, what should stay private, what must be checked, and who is responsible for the final decision.

The goal is not to make a perfect document in one pass. The goal is to make the teacher decision clear enough that another educator can inspect it, reuse it, or challenge it without needing a private explanation.
