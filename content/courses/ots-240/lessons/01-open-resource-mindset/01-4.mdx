# Checkpoint Review

## Readiness Rubric

- **Ready**: all artifacts have source, license, allowed-use rule, and review date.
- **Revise**: at least one row lacks attribution or privacy rule.
- **Blocked**: missing licenses for any non-self-created item.

## Check to run

- Verify at least three rows in the log.
- Verify one row has `private use only` with a student reference.
- Verify one row has `CC BY 4.0` and clear attribution.

If all checks pass, move to Chapter 02.
