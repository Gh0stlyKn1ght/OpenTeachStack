# Chapter Plan

## Why This Matters

A shared curriculum grows faster with contribution habits, and stays safer with review habits.
You need one lightweight review workflow.

## Chapter Goal

Build a contribution checklist with four explicit checks:
- scope,
- clarity,
- privacy,
- revertability.

## Checklist Setup

Add a `Contribution checklist` section to your process notes:

- who requested change,
- why change needed,
- files touched,
- expected review outcome,
- date + reviewer.
