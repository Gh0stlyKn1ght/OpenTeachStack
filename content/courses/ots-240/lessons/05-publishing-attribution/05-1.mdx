# Source Notes and Attribution

## Why This Matters

Attribution is not a footer task.
It is a teaching practice that preserves trust.

## Required Source Note Columns

- source name,
- URL or document path,
- license,
- specific credit,
- modified by,
- date collected.

## Example

| Source | Type | License | Credit | Note |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| RoboticsSafetyGuide | PDF | CC BY-SA 4.0 | Open Robotics Team | adapted visuals |
| Math Rubric Template | Internal | CC BY 4.0 | You | renamed sections |

## Rule

Every non-original asset must include a credit record before repo publish.
