# Choosing a License

## Why This Matters

The repository license governs what teachers may do with your work.
Choose one that matches your classroom goals.

## Decision Pattern

1. Do you want derivatives?
2. Is commercial reuse okay?
3. Should derivatives stay open?

## Practical Choices

- `CC BY 4.0` if you want broad educational reuse.
- `CC BY-SA 4.0` if you want derivative sharing to stay open.
- `CC BY-NC` only if avoiding commercial reuse is essential.

## Evidence

Record selected license in `LICENSE` file and mention it in README.
