## Safety Notes

- AI-generated visuals must be checked for accuracy, bias, misleading details, and classroom fit.
- Factual diagrams need trusted sources or teacher-created structure.
- Published materials should disclose AI-generated visuals when used.
- Do not use copyrighted, student-identifiable, or policy-restricted media without permission.
