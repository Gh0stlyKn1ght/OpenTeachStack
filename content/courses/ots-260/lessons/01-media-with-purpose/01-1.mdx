# Media as Explanation, Not Decoration

## Why This Matters

When media explains a step, students use it.
When media only decorates, students ignore it.

## What to Evaluate

For each asset, answer:

- What misconception does this correct?
- Which question can students answer after seeing it?
- Where would this fail if removed?

## Quick Exercise

Take your robotics safety map and choose one asset.

| Asset | Purpose | Test Question | Keep? |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arm wiring flowchart | explain order of checks | “Which step comes first?” | yes |
| Team celebration image | engage team mood | no learning question | no |
| Safety timer graphic | guide pacing | “How long for each station?” | yes |

## Action Rule

Keep only media that answers a classroom question.

Discard or archive decoration-only assets from your release pack.

## Mini-Task

Create a two-column review:

| Candidate asset | Decision |
| --- | --- |
| [asset name] | keep / replace / remove |

Document the first one in `media-purpose-review.md`.

## Quality Check

- At least one kept asset has a direct learner question.
- At least one removed/replaced item has a replacement reason.
