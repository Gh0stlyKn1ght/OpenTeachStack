# Accessibility Before Polish

## Why This Matters

Accessible media helps more students and survives teacher handoffs.

## Default Accessibility Checklist

Before polishing visuals, validate:

- alt text present and meaningful,
- captions or transcript available,
- high-contrast colors,
- readable font size,
- no unnecessary animation for core information.

## Sample Audit Table

| Asset | Alt text | Captions | Color contrast | Final action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Safety flowchart | Yes | N/A | high | keep |
| Procedure slide deck | Yes | Planned | medium | revise |
| Short clip script | N/A | Transcript | N/A | revise |

## Accessibility Guardrail

Do not ship any asset without:
- at least one accessibility pass checkbox,
- a reviewer name,
- and an agreed remediation note when needed.

## Practice Task

Open your media asset list and add:
- one accessibility status per item,
- one remediation step for each "needs review."

## Quality Check

If two or more assets fail core checks, downgrade the chapter status to **revise** before moving forward.
