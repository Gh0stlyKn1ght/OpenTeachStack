# Build Task: Media Purpose Map

## Why This Matters

This is where planning turns into a tool your team can execute.

## Build Deliverable

Create the final `Media purpose map` with:
- objective,
- chosen media,
- reason,
- accessibility status,
- owner,
- update date.

## Template

| Objective | Media type | File/Asset | Reason | Accessibility status | Owner | Review date |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Safety order check | Diagram | `media/flow-safety-v1.png` | Sequence clarity | alt+contrast passed | You | 2026-09-22 |
| Timing reminder | Slide | `slides/lab-timing.md` | Reduces lost time | no captions | pending |
| Procedure support | Short text/audio | `script/safety-checklist.md` | Supports different learning modes | transcript added | You | 2026-09-22 |

## Completion Rules

- mark any `no`/`pending` item with a due-date fix,
- one item must be marked `ready` after accessibility validation,
- include one peer-review confirmation row.

## Reflection

If a reviewer asks what this map proves, they should say: “every asset is tied to an instructional need and tested for accessibility.”
