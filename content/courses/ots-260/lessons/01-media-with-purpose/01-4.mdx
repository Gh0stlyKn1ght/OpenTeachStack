# Checkpoint Review

## Why This Matters

You now prove the chapter is complete through evidence, not description.

## Readiness Rubric

- **Ready**: every required media has learning purpose and accessibility status.
- **Revise**: at least one media decision has no purpose or fails access check.
- **Blocked**: student-identifying info appears in a shared asset.

## Evidence Checklist

- Completed media purpose map (5+ entries).
- At least one accessibility fix ticket is logged.
- One item explicitly marked “replace/remove.”
- One reviewer acknowledgment row.

## Final Exit Decision

If status is `Ready`, move to Chapter 02.
If `Revise`, update the map and rerun this checkpoint.
