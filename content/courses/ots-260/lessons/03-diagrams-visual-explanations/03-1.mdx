# Flowcharts, Timelines, and Systems Maps

## Core Idea

Diagrams and Visual Explanations is about turning a process, comparison, system, or misconception into a diagram students can actually use. In this section, **Flowcharts, Timelines, and Systems Maps** turns that focus into a practical authoring move for one lesson.

The useful question is not "Can I make this look impressive?" The useful question is "Will this media help students understand, practice, review, or revisit the learning target?" A teacher should leave this section with a visible piece of the chapter artifact: **Diagram set**.
