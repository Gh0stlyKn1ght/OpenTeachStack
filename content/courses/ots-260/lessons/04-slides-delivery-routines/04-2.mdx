# Student-Facing Directions

## Why This Matters

If students need extra interpretation from the teacher voice every slide, the deck is not yet instructionally complete.

## Core Idea

Write directions for every interactive slide using one sentence + one verb rule:

- What to do
- Where to do it
- What success looks like

Avoid ambiguity words: `fix this`, `try it`, `do your best`.

## Directions Template

Use this sentence pattern:

`On this slide, [action verb] [specific thing] in [tool/space] and [quality check].`

Example:
`On this slide, open the shared document and label one cause and one effect, then compare your pair’s labels with the board.`

## Accessibility Note

Directions should be understandable without audio alone and without color-only cues.
