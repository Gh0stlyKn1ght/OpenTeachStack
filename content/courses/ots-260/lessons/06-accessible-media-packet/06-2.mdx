# Peer Review and Revision

## Why This Matters

Independent review catches failures the original creator may miss.

## Core Idea

Run one accessibility-focused pass with one peer who did not build the content.

## Review Pass

Ask your reviewer to score each item:
- Are actions understandable without color?
- Are text cues explicit?
- Are captions synchronized and accurate?

## Practice Task

Add two review rows in `Accessible lesson media packet`:

| Reviewer | Area reviewed | Result | Required fix |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [name] | [images/videos/slides] | READY/REVISE | [one fix] |

## Reflection

What feedback did you get that you would not have noticed alone?
