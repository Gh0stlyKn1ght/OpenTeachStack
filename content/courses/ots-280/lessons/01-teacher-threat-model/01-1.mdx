# What Is a Threat Model?

## Core Idea

Teacher Threat Model is about mapping realistic teacher risks without turning safety work into fear-based noise. In this section, **What Is a Threat Model?** turns that chapter focus into one practical teacher safety habit.

The goal is not to make teachers paranoid or technical for its own sake. The goal is to help a teacher decide what actually needs protection and what can be ignored for now and leave behind a safe, maintainable artifact: **Teacher personal risk map**.
