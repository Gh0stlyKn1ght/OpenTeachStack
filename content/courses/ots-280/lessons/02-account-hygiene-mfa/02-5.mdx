# Build Task: Password Manager and MFA Checklist

## Core Idea

This build task turns chapter 2 work into a reusable operating document.  
The checklist is where your inventory, recovery checks, and MFA decisions become part of your routine.

## Do This

- Create a table with columns:
  - account name (sanitized),
  - unique password confirmed,
  - MFA enabled,
  - recovery method updated,
  - last reviewed,
  - next action.
- Add entries for each account group from your inventory.
- For each entry, choose one concrete action due within 30 days.
- Add a private notes row for details you do not share in review copies.
- Choose a review cadence (for example monthly or each term break).

## Evidence of Completion

- A checklist exists with at least six account rows.
- At least one account row moves from “not set” to “secured” during the chapter.
- Recovery status is reviewed and updated for at least three entries.
- A dated review cadence is documented.
- A private details annex is identified so sensitive fields are not shared publicly.

## Safety Check

- Do not enter live passwords, recovery answers, security questions, or backup codes.
- Do not publish full email addresses, usernames, or phone numbers in shared versions.
- If a row reveals unresolved compromise risk, escalate according to school policy before sharing a public action plan.
- Maintain a private version for sensitive operational details.

## Reflection

Which checklist row will you trust to keep you safe if you are away from school for a month?
