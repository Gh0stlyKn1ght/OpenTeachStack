# Chapter Checkpoint

## Core Idea

This is the transition point.  
You now decide what is secure enough to move on from, and what still needs a short follow-up before continuing to chapter 03.

## Do This

- Re-open your inventory and checklist.
- Identify the three highest-impact accounts and mark the next maintenance step for each.
- Verify that every highest-impact account has:
  - a safe password strategy,
  - a current recovery option,
  - a chosen MFA setting.
- Write one short commitment for your next two weeks.

## Evidence of Completion

- Three high-impact accounts are marked with explicit maintenance steps.
- At least one follow-up owner and date is recorded for each step.
- One checkpoint note states readiness to start chapter 03, or clearly identifies one blocker.
- The checkpoint includes what can be shared publicly versus what stays private.

## Safety Check

- Do not share complete credentials or account identifiers.
- Redact real personal information before sharing checkpoint notes.
- If you detected a potential active threat, escalate through school channels and stop course progression until guidance is received.
