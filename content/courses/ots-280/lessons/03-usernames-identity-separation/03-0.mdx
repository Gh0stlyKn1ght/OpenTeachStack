# Chapter Overview

## Core Idea

Usernames and Identity Separation is about separating personal, school, and public educator identity signals when connection creates unnecessary exposure. In this section, **Chapter Overview** turns that chapter focus into one practical teacher safety habit.

The goal is not to make teachers paranoid or technical for its own sake. The goal is to help a teacher decide which usernames, photos, bios, or contact paths should stay connected or separate and leave behind a safe, maintainable artifact: **Public/private identity map**.
