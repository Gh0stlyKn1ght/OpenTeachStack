# Auditing Public Profiles

## Core Idea

Social Media and Doxxing Risk is about reducing public profile details that reveal location, routines, family information, students, or unsafe contact paths. In this section, **Auditing Public Profiles** turns that chapter focus into one practical teacher safety habit.

The goal is not to make teachers paranoid or technical for its own sake. The goal is to help a teacher decide what public details should be kept, revised, removed, or reviewed against policy and leave behind a safe, maintainable artifact: **Public profile audit**.
