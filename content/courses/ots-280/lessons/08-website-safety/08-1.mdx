# Personal and Student Exposure Risks

## Core Idea

Website Safety for Teacher Sites is about checking teacher sites for private files, student exposure, unsafe forms, embeds, downloads, contact paths, and tracking. In this section, **Personal and Student Exposure Risks** turns that chapter focus into one practical teacher safety habit.

The goal is not to make teachers paranoid or technical for its own sake. The goal is to help a teacher decide what should be public, private, revised, removed, or escalated before sharing and leave behind a safe, maintainable artifact: **Teacher website security audit**.
