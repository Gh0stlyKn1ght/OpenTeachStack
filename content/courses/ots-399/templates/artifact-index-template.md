## Status Rules

- Draft: useful but not ready for release.
- Ready: reviewed for source, privacy, accessibility, and maintenance.
- Queued: intentionally unfinished and named in the release note.

## Review Prompts

- Does this artifact support the capstone purpose?
- Can another teacher find it without hidden context?
- Is private material kept out of public routes, files, downloads, and repos?
- Are source, license, prompt, and revision notes attached or queued?
