# OTS-399 Peer Review Protocol

Use this protocol before final capstone release.

## Reviewer Instructions

Review the capstone as a teacher who may need to teach, reuse, maintain, or audit the system later.

## Questions

- What teaching need does the system solve?
- Can you follow the artifact index and system map?
- Which artifact is most useful?
- Which artifact is confusing, missing, or unnecessary?
- Are sources, licenses, prompts, and revision notes visible enough?
- Is any public material unsafe, inaccessible, or too private?
- What should be revised before release?
