## Required Notes

- What does the component do?
- What data does it touch?
- How was it tested without student-identifiable information?
- What command, route, screenshot, or manual check proves it worked?
- How can it be revised, removed, or rolled back?

## Blockers

Do not mark a component ready if it touches live student data, credentials, hidden assessment material, private district systems, or public publishing settings without review.
