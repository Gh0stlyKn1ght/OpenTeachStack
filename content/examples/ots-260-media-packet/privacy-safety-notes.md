# Privacy, Safety, and Copyright Notes

## Data safety
- Use fictional students and fake names only.
- No real student data, no private identifiers.
- No screenshots containing private information.

## Accessibility
- Include readable text labels and alt-text equivalents.
- Keep action steps short and screen-reader friendly.
- Avoid color-only instructions.

## Licensing
- Prefer open-licensed resources with clear attributions.
- Record source quality and reuse limits in notes.
