Use this Library page when collecting tool candidates for a lesson, course site, or student workflow.

## Workflow

Check purpose, privacy, access, export options, accessibility, and maintenance.

## What to verify

Free does not automatically mean safe, accessible, or sustainable.

## Common mistakes

- Choosing novelty over classroom fit.
- Forgetting account and age requirements.
