# What this knowledge base is

## The real problem

Teachers do not always need a full course. Sometimes they need the prompt, checklist, source, or workflow that helps tomorrow.

## Quick answer

The KB is the problem-first layer. Courses stay structured and video-friendly. Prompts, templates, sources, safety checks, workflows, examples, and tools all live under the Knowledge Base.

## When to use this

Use the KB when you need a workflow, safety check, template reference, or troubleshooting path.

## Workflow

1. Name the real teaching problem.
2. Find the matching KB article.
3. Copy or adapt the linked Knowledge Base asset.
4. Verify sources, privacy, and classroom fit.
5. Save what worked so it can become a system.

## What to verify

Check that the advice matches your course, students, local policy, and available tools.

## Common mistakes

- Treating a KB article like a full course.
- Copying a template without teacher judgment.
- Skipping source and privacy checks.
