# Quiz and Checkpoint Plan

## Suggested Checkpoints

1. **Chapter close checkpoints** after each module in the course.
2. **Artifact verification checks** after each major artifact entry.
3. **Peer-review mini-check** before final chapter submission.

## Question Types

- Scenario checks (What would you do if...)
- Evidence selection questions (Which artifact proves...)
- Safety and privacy checks (Which item is safe to publish now)

## Mastery Checks

- A lesson artifact is accepted when it includes: student context, evidence, review step, and revision trigger.
- Require one correction cycle before allowing final release.

## Retake / Revision

Learners revise at checkpoints until evidence and review notes are complete.
